Trends in biblical quotations

The heart of this site is a set of interactive visualizations which let you see the trend in the rate of quotations for any given verse in Chronicling America and Nineteenth-century Newspapers, then see examples of those quotations on the newspaper page.

If you have a verse you want to look up directly, you can search for it. Note that the lists of verses below are selective, and so return verses with higher quality results, but the verse search feature below returns nearly every verse, and the quality of the results for some verses may be lower.

{{ partial "verse-select.html" }}

Below are the trends for featured verses, some of which are also the subject of the verse histories. You can also browse the verses in three other ways: (1) the most quoted verses in descending order of frequency; (2) verses that had a peak of quotation in a given year, arranged chronologically; and (3) frequently quoted verses in their order in the Christian Bible.
